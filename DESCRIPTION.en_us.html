Matemasie is an ultra-bold display typeface characterized by rounded edges and a distinctive feature where the shapes widen towards the top. It draws inspiration from the Adinkra symbol "Mate Masie," meaning "what I hear, I keep." This symbolizes interconnected ears, emphasizing the importance of listening and communication in oral traditions and cultures.

Additionally, Mate Masie embodies principles of wisdom and prudence.

Adam Yeo ,a Type and Graphic Designer originally from Cote d'Ivoire and currently based in France, conceived Matemasie with guidance and technical assistance from mentors Lisa Huang and Nathan Willis.
